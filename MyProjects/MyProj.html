SpringCinema

Edit Project

An application for the management of a cinema in Java, using the Spring framework.

  • Tasks

    The application must be able to organize the cinema as follows: everyday, there is a program. Each movie begins at a certain hour and has a duration. Clients must be able to buy tickets for movies online, they can choose seats and will receive a memento 15 minutes before the starting hour of the movie. The ticket will be received through e-mail if there are free places.

  • Plan

    The student must present the prototype of the graphical interface until the 26th of March. The architecture of the interface shall be presented until the 30th of April. The entire project shall be presented at the beginning of July.

  • Recomandations
    • Download IntelliJ Ultimate and register as a student
    • Install Maven
    • Use LaTeX for writing the documentation (I recommend TexMaker)

AR Museum

Edit Project

Make museum visitations more exciting with this AR Android application.

  • Tasks

    Take museums to the next level using a simple android phone camera. Each exponate will have a code that can be scanned with the camera. When the exponate is detected, the application will offer more information and an entire gallery of photos, movies and MP3 files with that specific exponate. Also, the app will have a location detection service, in order to detect the museum you are visiting in that certain moment.

  • Plan

    The student must present the prototype of the graphical interface until the 26th of March. The architecture of the interface shall be presented until the 30th of April. The entire project shall be presented at the beginning of July.

    • Download Unity and the VForia Plugin
    • Use LaTeX for writing the documentation (I recommend TexMaker)
